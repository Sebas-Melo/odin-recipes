Picanha

Description: Picanha is a cut of beef first made popular by Brazil, and currently considered one of the top tastiest beef cuts worldwide. This cut is visually recognized by it's fat layer that, taste-wise, brings an unbelievable flavour to this piece. Its combination of flavorful taste and tenderness makes this cut an essential in every Brazilian and Portuguese Churrasco.

Ingredients

  • 1 full cut of picanha
  • salt

Steps

  1. Sear both sides of the meat in high heat. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Cut the picanha in thin steaks so that every steak as a side with the fat layer. Grill the steaks on both sides in high heat.
  3. When the picanha is at the desired cooking point let it rest aside for 2 to 3 minutes and sprinkle it with salt.
